Move

context & brief/

The curves on the poster are meant to evoke the physical movements of the human body, and the overall design is intended to convey a sense of energy and vitality.

Colors/

The black and white color scheme is not only visually striking, but also serves to emphasize the movement and curves on the poster. The lack of color allows the viewer to focus more fully on the dynamic shapes and lines, and creates a sense of simplicity and clarity.
