Timeline

October 2024 Project Shop Floor

Developed and optimized a shop floor project using advanced data analysis and 3D Deep Learning. This project involves using 3D Deep Learning architectures such as PointNet, Clustering to detect and classify 3D objects such as Humans and generate insights into the ShopFloor in Arena2036

August 2024 Represented Pakistan at Research Symposium Canada

As Part of the MITACS GRI Program. Was the only Pakistani at the University of Alberta to deliver at the research symposium recieving recognition from their head of international relationships. Infront of over 200 faculty and students.

June–September 2024 MITACS GRI Internship

Selected for MITACS Internship in Canada at the University of Alberta. Internship worth over 10 K CAD. Under the Supervision of Professor Bin Han and Professor Michelle, I worked in the domain of analysing their framework to resolve border artifacts in images using JPEG 2000 standard based on wavelet transform.

June 2024 Published Research Paper (SCAP)

Published a research paper as a First Author in SCAP, on 2D Critical region Identification using Images based on SSIM. This paper was the culmination of the Convergence progject.

May 2024 Project 3D Synthetic Data Generation

To tackle lack of 3D Data with defects (Given the limited 3D Defected Data). Working in developing 3D Synthetic Data consisting of Manufactured Parts with induced defects using Generative Adversarial Network (GAN) based approach.

Feburary 2024 Convergence

Presented research on convergence and AI applications at an international conference, earning recognition for innovation.

December 2023 Defect Detection Research Paper

First Author of Research Paper marking the culmination of the Defect Detection Project

September 2023 ML Lead at NUST Airworks

Led machine learning initiatives at NUST Airworks, focusing on the development of AI-driven systems for aerial vehicles. Mentored over 40 students in the domain of Defect Detection. Obtained Performance award at Technofest Turkey.

March 2023 Poster Presentation

Co-Author of a Poster Presentation on Optimising Forming Process Behaviour using Artifiical Intelligence.

Feburary 2023 Defect Detection Project

Project involved Identification and Classification of 3D Manufacturing Defects (Wrinkles, Bridges and Gaps), in collaboration with FARO and Boeing. This involved using a combination of Spectral Approaches (Wavelet Transform) and Ai based optimzation to classify defects given their point clouds. Hence the framework developed provides point level accuracy.

August 2022 Represented Pakistan at Technofest

Represented Pakistan on a global platform at Technofest Turkey. This global competition involves participants from over 90 countries to compete in the development of UAV technology. My role involved development and integration of the Hardware and Software of the HexaCopter Developed by my team.
